He then received in 2019 from the United States Agency for International Development, a grant to works develop its demonstrated knowledge in leadership and entrepreneurship. After that, he was promoted as Bioinformatician engineer at the Institut de Recherche pour le Developpement France. In October 2019, he began its Ph.D. studies in Bioinformatics with [Dr. Koua Dominique](https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Dominique_Koua), [Dr. Sébastien Dutertres](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=3sKU-9oAAAAJ&hl=en), and [Prof Adolphe Zeze](https://loop.frontiersin.org/people/346157/overview).
